The Story of My Life

Me, Myself, and I

I was born on November 11, 2006 at a hospital in Bulacan. I am both an introvert and an extrovert. I'm a night owl, I don't know, but I'm more energetic at night, than I am at daytime. I always do my homeworks/ activities at night because that's when my brain works best. I like sweets and vintage things. I like staring at the moon, and space out.
